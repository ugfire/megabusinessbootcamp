Our Background
The MEGA BUSINESS BOOTCAMP/Incubator Entrepreneurs programme in Uganda, was started by our founder mentor, CHRISTOPHER LULE KABOGERE, with an aim of transforming Uganda’s economy through entrepreneurial mentorship projects cord-named THE BUSINESSMIND airing every week on all National Televisions, Youtube channels {the businessmind, DITE PRODUCTION} and other other social networks. Our recent past seminars of 2021 and 2022 at KatiKati restaurant have been overwhelmingly supported by NTV UG, SALT TV, SPARK, BBS and all leading YouTube channels. These successful Mega bootcamps attracted 3,500 business people SMEs in physical attendance and 2 million Ugandans watching online. We have therefore been involved and interacted with over 100,000 small and medium enterprises operated by both young adults between 18-35 for the last 5 years, helping them with Entry strategies, sales strategies, marketing strategies, human resource development, fundraising, partnerships, networking, business compliance, business model development, access to cheap capital and exit strategies like business take-overs, mergers and Acquisition, total buy-outs, franchising, licensing and sell-outs. As an entity we have successfully run an incubator-like programme to nurture, grow and support SMEs to find, take advantage of all business opportunities locally and globally. Over the years we have identified several business challenges faced by each entrepreneur and we chose to create impactful solutions. We have closely worked with 15,000 businesses, helping them to access cheap capital, digitize their business operations. We are therefore looking forward to becoming a conduit pipeline for the entire industry of both small and medium until they cross-over to large corporations. This has always been our only goal since day one to build a coherent business environment for local entrepreneurs to excel and enhance employment opportunities for the youth. We therefore intend to partner with several organizations to achieve our goals. With such a business following we have garnered over the years has encouraged us to leverage and seek partnership with you to achieve our vision of mentoring one million entrepreneurs and ensure we create THE UGANDA ENTREPRENEURS BUSINESS FUND(UEBF) to help these businesses that are potentially promising cross-over to the other end of Large companies, this will eventually lead to a health economy that is self-sustaining.
